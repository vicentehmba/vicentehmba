## About me

I’m a software developer interested in exploring new technology and turning it into practical products.

I enjoy working through unfamiliar problems, experimenting with new platforms, and understanding how emerging tools can enable solutions that were previously difficult or impractical to build.

My interests currently span AI systems, developer tools, web and mobile applications, product design, and technology for underserved markets.

## Current focus

I’m currently building **Zencode**, an AI coding workspace that brings models, agents, tools, terminals, and development workflows into one environment.

At its center is **ZEN**, an orchestration runtime designed to prioritize model-native capabilities while also supporting external agents and integrations through ACPs and SDKs.

Alongside Zencode, I regularly experiment with mobile applications, AI-assisted products, financial technology, creator tools, and other ideas shaped by the changing technology landscape.
